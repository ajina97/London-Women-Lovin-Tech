About London Women Lovin' Tech

We wish to inspire independent, confident women who do not stay behind the scenes or lay low rather raise their voices to state opinions and goals without fear. We spread the message of ‘Never back Down’ and hope that it makes a difference.

Our belief

It has been proven that woman’s contribution leads to higher success rates and returns and merely curbing it is unsustainable and illogical. If helped in the right way especially through mediums like mentoring and with the adequate amount of resources, a lot of new ideas can be nurtured. We try to keep narrowing down ways that will encourage more participation for we believe that the woman have a lot of skills and capabilities that they can bring on board.

Our Mission

At London Women Lovin' Tech, we have decided to dedicate ourselves for the betterment of women’s place in the entrepreneur and business world of technology in UK through a thorough understanding of all aspects and perspectives.

Our vision

London Women Lovin' Tech envisions a society that gives equal rights, representation and encouragement to women in the technology world such that they can use their capabilities to the maximum.

The Future

As predicted by researches and surveys, in the next ten years, UK tech industries would be working hard to become as mixed in their members as possible. Every woman should have the right to pick what she wants to pursue. We will engage in as many activities and campaigns such that our support can boost the motion faster. We hope to partner with many influential women and have tie ups with various IT industries so that as a unit we can bring forth glorious changes to the society of UK.
